About us

One of Asia's foremost extraordinary garden destinations.
Gardens by the Bay opened its doors in June 2012 , becoming the nation's masterpiece of horticulture and garden artistry.
The Gardens is made up of different plants that are not commonly seen in this part of the world, showcasing the plant kingdom in an astounding manner that entertains and educates visitors.
